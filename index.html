About

I am dedicated to harnessing the power of data science for the public good.

In May 2026, I will graduate with a Master’s in Geographic Information Science from Clark University, where I have focused on geospatial analysis, remote sensing, and community planning. Prior to graduate school, I worked in education and community-based roles, designing comprehensive curriculums and engaging a variety of audiences in training programs. I graduated cum laude from Columbia University with a degree in Urban Studies/Architecture.

With a combination of technical and educational experience, I excel at communicating data-driven insights and developing accessible tools.

I am actively seeking high-impact jobs at the intersection of technology and civic engagement.
